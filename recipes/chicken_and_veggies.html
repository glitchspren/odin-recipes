Crispy Sheet Pan Chicken and Veggies

This one-pan meal is a simple and flavorful way to get dinner on the table with minimal cleanup. By roasting the chicken and vegetables together, they cook evenly and take on a delicious, crispy texture.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts
  • 1 chopped broccoli crown
  • 1 chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 sliced yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Chop the chicken and vegetables into bite-sized pieces and place them on a large baking sheet.
  3. Drizzle everything with olive oil and sprinkle with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Spread the mixture in an even layer on the baking sheet to ensure even cooking and browning.
  5. Roast for 20–25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender with slightly crispy edges. Serve immediately.
